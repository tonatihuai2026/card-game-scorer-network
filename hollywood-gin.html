How Hollywood Gin Scoring Works

Multiple simultaneous games
In Hollywood Gin, each pair of players tracks several "games" at once (often 3), each starting and ending at a different hand. This calculator simplifies that to one running total per player — track each game's column separately on paper if you need the full multi-game layout, and use this for the overall running total.
Knock vs. Gin vs. Undercut
A regular knock scores the winner's deadwood difference; a gin (no deadwood) adds a 25-point bonus; an undercut (defender ties or beats the knocker's deadwood) flips the bonus to the defender instead. Enter whatever net point total results per hand.
