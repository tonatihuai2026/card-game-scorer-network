Pinochle Score Tracker

Running Score

Enter each team's total points for the hand (bid result, melds, and tricks combined) and add it to the running score. Everything runs in your browser — nothing you type is sent anywhere.

How Pinochle Scoring Works

Meld
Points scored before play for card combinations (runs, marriages, aces around, etc.), counted only if the bidding team makes their bid.
Tricks
Points from cards captured during play (aces=11, tens=10, kings=4, queens=3, jacks=2), plus 10 for the last trick. Total trick points across all teams sum to 250 in a standard deck.
Bidding
The bidding team must reach their bid total (meld + tricks) or they score zero (or negative, the bid amount) for the hand — house rules vary, so this calculator just takes whatever final point total you enter per team.
